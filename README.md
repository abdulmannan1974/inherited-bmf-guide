A comprehensive, interactive learning guide for **Inherited Aplastic Anaemia and Bone Marrow Failure Syndromes** designed for haematologists, physicians, and trainees.

### Features

- **Complete coverage** of Chapter 30 from Hoffbrand's Postgraduate Haematology (8th Edition)
- **Interactive diagrams** including FA-BRCA pathway, telomere biology, ribosome biogenesis
- **Comprehensive tables** with all genetic subtypes and clinical features
- **High-yield callouts** and exam pearls for rapid learning
- **Clinical algorithms** for diagnosis and treatment
- **Responsive design** - works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices

### Topics Covered

1. **Fanconi Anaemia (FA)** - DNA repair defects, 22 genes, clinical features, treatment
2. **Dyskeratosis Congenita (DC)** - Telomere biology disorders, 20+ genes, mucocutaneous triad
3. **Shwachman-Diamond Syndrome (SDS)** - Ribosome biogenesis, pancreatic insufficiency
4. **Diamond-Blackfan Anaemia (DBA)** - Ribosomopathy, pure red cell aplasia
5. **Congenital Dyserythropoietic Anaemia (CDA)** - Types I, II (HEMPAS), III
6. **Congenital Neutropenias** - SCN, Kostmann syndrome, cyclical neutropenia
7. **CAMT & TAR** - Syndromic thrombocytopenias
8. **New/Overlapping Syndromes** - GATA2, RUNX1, DDX41, etc.

### Usage
